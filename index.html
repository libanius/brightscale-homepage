Legal basics

Privacy Policy (summary)

BrightScale collects the information you send through our forms (such as name, contact details, project description and service preferences) so we can match you with professionals and operate our platform.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your project details with a limited number of professionals when it is necessary to connect you with potential providers.

Terms of Use (summary)

BrightScale is a lead-generation and marketplace platform. We introduce homeowners to independent professionals, but we do not provide the services ourselves and are not a party to the contract between you and the pro. You are responsible for reviewing licenses, insurance and agreements with any provider.

Lead & payment policy (summary)

Contractors pay BrightScale for access to lead information. Refunds or credits, when applicable, are evaluated on a case-by-case basis according to our future full lead policy. Homeowners are never charged for submitting a request on the public website.
